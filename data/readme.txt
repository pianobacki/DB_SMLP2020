DataImageFix.rda

Data from scene viewing experiment. 
participants viewed 60 images under 3 different stimulus interaction conditions. 1x3 design

1 Silent Counting
2 Point At Last Counted Person (after image presentation)
3 Loud Counting (with hand gestures)


DataImageFix.rda: fixational events during image presentation 


columns 

VP	subject number
trial	trial number
Img	image number
cond	1-"SilentCounting" 2-"PointAtLastCountedPerson" 4-"LoudCounting"
fd fixation duration in ms
DV dependent variable (here fd)
